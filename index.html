Photo Rater

See how many score you can get with your photo Upload a photo or take one from camera and let this app calculate a score for you.

Key Features • Upload photo from photo gallery or camera • Recognize information from uploaded photo as tag • Calculate total score from each given tag • Share your photo score with your friend on Facebook
